Who We Are

Built On Solid Ground

Taffline Bricks is a Zimbabwe-based construction materials supplier committed to delivering quality products at competitive prices. Based in Harare, we serve clients across the country — from individual home builders to large commercial contractors.

Our range covers everything you need on a construction site: bricks, pavers, cement, timber, sand, quarry stones and more. We also offer grabber and tipper truck hire for efficient on-site logistics and delivery.
